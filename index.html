My path into GRC came from two places: years working in IT, where I learned how systems actually behave, and years as a corporate trainer, where I learned how to make technical things legible to the people who don't live inside them. In compliance work, both matter. Controls nobody understands won't get followed. Policies written like legal boilerplate won't get implemented. I know how to build the thing, and I know what it has to do. Right now I am focused on the German market, mapping ISO 27001 controls to the BSI IT-Grundschutz compendium in Verinice, working toward TELC C1 German, and finishing my MSc in Computer Science at Heriot-Watt University.
